‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take and will it disrupt farming operations? +
Installation typically takes 2-3 weeks with minimal disruption. We coordinate with your farming schedule and work around critical operations. Most work is done during off-peak hours, and normal irrigation continues throughout the process.
What happens if sensors are damaged by farm equipment? +
Our sensors are designed to be robust and are installed with protective housings. If damage occurs, replacement sensors are covered under warranty for the first year, and we offer rapid replacement (within 24 hours) thereafter.
Can the system integrate with my existing irrigation controls? +
Yes! Our system is designed to work with most existing irrigation infrastructure. We provide integration APIs and can connect with popular irrigation control systems. Manual override capabilities are always maintained.
What training is provided and how technical is the system? +
We provide 4 hours of comprehensive on-site training, plus online resources and ongoing support. The mobile app is designed to be user-friendly - if you can use a smartphone, you can operate our system. No technical background required.
What happens if internet connectivity is poor on my farm? +
Our system uses multiple connectivity options including 4G, LoRaWAN, and satellite backup. Data is stored locally and synchronized when connectivity is available. The system continues to monitor and can provide alerts even with intermittent connectivity.
